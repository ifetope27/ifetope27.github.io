    About

    I'm Ifeoluwa Temitope Agunbiade — a Data Analyst passionate about transforming raw numbers into clear, actionable business insights. I specialize in Excel, SQL, Power BI, Python, ETL processes, data cleaning, and dashboard development, with hands-on experience building analytical solutions that support smarter decision-making and operational efficiency.

    I've worked on projects involving Sales Analysis, Logistics & Fleet Analysis, Financial Reporting, Customer Analysis, and interactive Power BI dashboards, where I developed strong problem-solving, reporting, and data visualization skills. I enjoy uncovering patterns in complex datasets and translating them into meaningful stories that businesses can use to improve performance and drive growth.

    Beyond technical expertise, I bring analytical thinking, attention to detail, adaptability, and effective communication to every project. I thrive in collaborative remote environments, continuously learning new tools and improving my ability to deliver impactful, data-driven solutions.

    Currently, I'm seeking remote internship and entry-level opportunities in Data Analytics and Business Intelligence where I can contribute, grow professionally, and help organizations make confident decisions backed by data.

    To me, data is more than numbers — it's the foundation for smarter strategies, better experiences, and measurable business success.
